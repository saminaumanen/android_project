Welcome to Travel Journal

Here are some basic instructions on how to use this app.
In this application, you can:

  • Write an entry to your journal
    Use the postcard icon to write entry to your journal. Hold down the item to delete it.
  • Use the camera function to save images to your devices gallery
  • Use the map to locate yourself and pin the latest place you've visited.
  • The map shows your current location as a blue dot.
